Welcome

In the Democratic Republic of Congo, life isn't always easy. Power surges and lack of water supply are a big challenge, making life very difficult, thus pushing one to be creative while researching for cheap but effective solutions. This is why I created ZambaWord, Zamba meaning forest, where I want to show people all around the world how to get around in regions where self sustaining becomes the only way to live an abundant life.
