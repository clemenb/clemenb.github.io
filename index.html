ZambaWorld's blog is all about DIY in DRCongo
In the Democratic Republic of Congo, life isn't always easy. Power surges and lack of water supply are a big challenge, making life very difficult, thus pushing one to be creative while researching the internet with the less possible means. This is why I created ZambaWord, Zamba meaning forest, where I want to show people all around the world how to get around in regions where self sustaining becomes the only way to live an abundant life.
En République démocratique du Congo, la vie n’est pas toujours facile. Les surtensions et le manque d’approvisionnement en eau constituent un défi de taille, rendant la vie très difficile, ce qui pousse à la créativité tout en cherchant sur Internet avec les moyens les moins possibles. C'est pourquoi j'ai créé ZambaWord, Zamba, une forêt, dans laquelle je veux montrer aux gens du monde entier comment se déplacer et vivre là où vous vivez.

How I pimped my gravel cleaner

In Part I of this video I'm exeplaining how to pimp the Eheim Quick Vac pro Automatic Gravel Cleaner because it's capacity to clean bigger fish tanks is kind of limited and its purpose is to be a hassle-free solution for small aquarium cleaning and quick spot maintenance. The weak suction force is to hinder the vac pro from sucking up gravel and maybe little fish as well. In my 600lt fish tank, I do not have gravel. So I made my vac pro more powerful for faster cleaning.

In part II of the video I'm showing you how even after almost two weeks, my Eheim vac pro still works fine with 12v input. The motor got no damage at all and is performing great, exactly what I need for my 600 Lt fish tank.

You will also see the underwater world of my fish tank that I filmed with my mobile phone.

How to grow tomatoes three different ways

In this video I'm showing you how I planted tomatoes three different ways; on concrete, in a barrel and in oil buckets.
Why just these three? Well I live in an old house that I turned into a construction site, and the plot in which this house is found is concreted almost everywhere. So I really don’t have space on the ground to plant vegetables.
This said, we will compare the results together and we'll see how to better plant tomatoes.
If you have comments, tips, guides, instructions or secrets on how to best grow big juicy tomatoes in our private garden, do not hesitate to share them with us. For my next video I will do some research in order to grow them larger. I did find some step by step instructions that I will share with you in a future tomato video :)
